## Lessons Learned

### 1. Archiving > Deleting

**Pattern**: Move to archive, don't delete
**Benefit**: Historical reference available
**Trade-off**: Slight disk space usage (negligible)

### 2. Date-Topic Organization

**Pattern**: `notes/archive/YYYY-MM-DD-topic/`
**Benefit**: Easy to find historical work by date or topic
**Scalability**: Works well for multiple projects over time

### 3. Prevention via .gitignore

**Pattern**: Explicitly ignore temporary file patterns
**Benefit**: Prevents future clutter automatically
**Example**: `*-WIP.md`, `*-SCRATCH.md`, etc.
